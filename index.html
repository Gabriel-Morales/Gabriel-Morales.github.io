Computer Scientist; PhD Student

I am a Ph.D. student working with focuses in security, privacy, and other processing methods using sensors and commodity devices. Specifically, my main area is in the Internet of Things (IoT) with some experience in networking, natural language processing (NLP), and broader machine learning (ML). These are all concepts I try to incorporate together to understand them better; I am an Apple geek at-heart, and enjoy making apps, trying out various frameworks and more within the Apple ecosystem. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing piano, or the ever-classic gaming.
